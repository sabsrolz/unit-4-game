Crystals Collector Game!

Welcome to the Crystals Collector Game! A score goal will be randomly generated at the beginning of each round. When you click on each of the four crystals, a number of points will also be randomly generated for each crystal. The purpose of the game is to attain the goal score, by adding up the individual points of each crystal every time they are clicked. The player wins when they match the current score to the goal. However, the player loses when the current score is higher than the goal. Press the reset button every time a round ends. Good luck!
